StitchXcel

Empowering Custom Clothing Experiences

A platform where innovation converges with fashion. Our endeavor bridges the gap between skilled tailors and discerning customers, offering a seamless experience in custom clothing. Collaborate with tailors, personalize orders, and explore tailor specialties to curate your unique fashion journey.

StitchXcel is a tailor and customer platform that empowers customers to place, update, and customize orders with their preferred tailors. Tailors can showcase their specialties and receive orders, completing them and delivering to customers.

Key Features

  • Customers can place, update, and delete orders
  • Customers can view tailor profiles and specialties
  • Online measurement submission
  • Customization options for designs

Novelty of StitchXcel

Experience the Future of Tailoring with StitchXcel!

StitchXcel revolutionizes the world of tailoring by introducing a platform that seamlessly connects customers and tailors like never before. Our platform is defined by its novel approach that transforms the entire tailoring experience:

  • Collaborative Personalization: StitchXcel fosters direct communication between customers and tailors, allowing for personalized orders and real-time updates.
  • Unleash Creativity: Embrace your unique style with customizable clothing designs, bringing your creative vision to life.
  • Discover Tailor Expertise: Easily find skilled tailors based on specialties, reviews, and emerging trends, empowering you to make informed choices.
  • Modern Measurement Submission: Say goodbye to traditional measurements; submit your measurements online for a perfect fit.
  • Simplified Order Management: Seamlessly manage your orders from start to finish, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free process.
  • Empowering Tailors: Tailors can showcase their skills through detailed profiles, expanding their reach and building a loyal clientele.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Python Flask, MongoDB
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, AJAX
  • ORM: Object-Relational Mapping

Hospital Management System

Transforming Healthcare Experience

Our Hospital Management System isn't just a software, it's a revolution in healthcare management. By seamlessly integrating cutting-edge technology with patient-centered care, we've redefined how patients connect with doctors and access medical services.

Key Features

  • Expert Profiles: Doctors can create comprehensive profiles, showcasing their expertise and specializations.
  • Easy Discovery: Patients can effortlessly find doctors based on location, hospital, and specific illnesses.
  • Efficient Search: A powerful search functionality allows patients to swiftly identify available doctors.
  • Convenient Appointments: Patients can conveniently book appointments with their preferred doctors, choosing suitable time slots.
  • Flexible Management: An advanced appointment management system empowers patients to modify or cancel appointments as needed.
  • Secure Payments: A secure online payment option provides a convenient way to pay for appointments.
  • Confirmation & Schedule: Upon successful booking, doctors receive immediate confirmation along with the appointment schedule.
  • Patient-Centric Care: Our patient-centric approach ensures a seamless and hassle-free healthcare experience for all.

Novelty

  • Empowered Doctors: Our platform empowers doctors to create intricate profiles showcasing their specialties and expertise, enabling them to stand out in the medical landscape.
  • Precise Discovery: Patients experience a new level of ease as they precisely discover doctors based on location, hospital affiliations, and specific illnesses.
  • Personalized Appointments: We put patients in control by offering them the ability to personally book appointments with their favored doctors, selecting optimal time slots.
  • Dynamic Management: Our flexible appointment management system gives patients the freedom to adjust or cancel appointments as their needs change.
  • Secure Transactions: With state-of-the-art online payment options, we provide patients with a secure and convenient method to settle appointment fees.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: C/C++, MySQL
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, AJAX
